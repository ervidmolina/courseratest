“My Interests”

From an early age human beings show certain interests, preferences and motivations about various jobs, activities and work contexts. Some people have a preference for jobs that require contact with nature, others for activities such as business, while others are interested in activities related to art, education, technology.

Therefore, interests can be defined as the degree of attraction for certain activities, people, objects or places, which depend on social, cultural and even age factors. Usually interests and preferences guide the future professional choice , the important thing is that interests are related to the skills and abilities needed to study and practice a profession. A person may say that he or she is interested in accounting or finance, but it is not enough with interest or liking for this type of activities, it is necessary that you have logical-mathematical abilities to perform adequately in this field.
